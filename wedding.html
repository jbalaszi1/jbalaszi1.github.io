Save The Date

I wanted Nikki and Miranda's save the date to be simple yet interesting. I used the colors red, black, and white because it makes the information on the card pop. The pictures are in black and white to also make it pop. It creates a certain simplistic definition of their love. I took the pictures as well.

I chose to do save the date and pictures for Nikki and Miranda because I wanted to experience designing from different aspects.
